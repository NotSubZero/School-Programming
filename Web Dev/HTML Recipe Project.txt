  <h1>Lemon Butter Salmon</h1>

  <h3>Equipment</h3>
  <ul>
    <li> Flat Spatula
    <li> Large Pan
    <li> Plates
  </ul>
  <h3>Ingredients</h3>
  <ul>
    <li> 2, 8 oz Salmon fillets (16 oz Total)
    <li> 2 Tbsp Butter
    <li> 1/2 Tbsp Minced Garlic
    <li> 2 Tsp of Salt
    <li> 2 Tsp Black Pepper
    <li> 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley
    <li> 1 Tbsp Basil
    <li> 1/2 Cup Sweet Bell Peppers
    <li> 1/4 Cup Lemon Juice
  </ul>
  <h3>Directions</h3>
  <ol>
    <li> Take Salmon Fillets in a bowl or plate and season with salt and pepper.
    <li> Place the room temperature (solid) butter in the pan.
    <li> After mixing around the butter for a few minutes, it will turn brown, at that point, place the minced 
      garlic in the middle of the pan and don't mix it for about half a minute.  
    <li> Now mix the garlic butter, you are free to place the fillets and parsley on the pan. 
      All of the remaining vegetables should be on the pan as well. 
    <li> After about 5 minutes, you can flip. Once the Salmon reaches a flaky consistency, you will know
      that it's done. 
    <li> Before taking it off the pan, pour the lemon juice on the Salmon and keep it on the pan for a minute.
    <li> All done!
  </ol>
